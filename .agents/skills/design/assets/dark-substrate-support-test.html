Support Ink Candidates for Amber Lead

Amber #c4813e is the lead ink. These three candidates compete for the support role — baseline, reference, underlying structure. Each is tested in Mode 3 dual-ink figures (where the pairing matters most) and as a Mode 2 single-ink figure (to verify the support reads on its own).

§ 1–2 show each support as single ink (scatter cloud, contour). § 3–5 show each support paired with amber (strat-dual, contour-dual, anno). § 6 shows a bar chart with support bars + amber focus.
